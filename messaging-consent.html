Messaging Consent

Messages are sent only to the account owner and known contacts who have directly provided their phone number and explicitly asked to receive low-volume personal assistant notifications, reminders, or requested updates.

Consent method

Consent is obtained directly through personal interaction with Mike Patraw. No purchased lists, scraped contacts, or unsolicited bulk messaging are used.

Opt out

Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP or by directly informing Mike Patraw that they no longer wish to receive messages.
