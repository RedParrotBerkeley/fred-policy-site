Fred Messaging Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to Fred messaging services operated by Mike Patraw for low-volume personal assistant notifications, reminders, direct updates, and support messages.

Information we collect

We may collect and maintain the following categories of information:

  • mobile phone number and messaging consent status
  • message history, including HELP, STOP, and other support or opt-out requests
  • name or contact context you voluntarily provide
  • basic delivery, timing, and support records needed to operate the messaging program

How information is used

Information is used only to operate the Fred messaging program, send requested notifications and reminders, respond to support requests, maintain compliance records, troubleshoot delivery issues, and protect service safety and integrity.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties except service providers and platforms required to operate, route, support, secure, and deliver the messaging service.

All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties, affiliates, or partners for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for service operation, recordkeeping, troubleshooting, safety, compliance, and enforcement of opt-out preferences.

Your choices

You may opt out of messaging at any time by replying STOP. You may request support by replying HELP where supported or by using the contact information provided in the program terms.

Contact

For privacy questions about Fred messaging, contact Mike Patraw through the communication channel where you enrolled or were originally contacted.
