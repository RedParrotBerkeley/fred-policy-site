Privacy Policy

This messaging service is operated by Mike Patraw for low-volume personal assistant notifications and direct messaging.

Information collected

We may collect a recipient's phone number, message history, and any information voluntarily provided in replies.

How information is used

Information is used only to deliver requested notifications, assistant updates, reminders, and related direct communications.

Sharing

Information is not sold. Information may be processed by service providers required to deliver messaging and assistant functionality.

Data retention

Message records may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary for service operation, troubleshooting, safety, and compliance.

Opt out

Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP or by directly informing Mike Patraw that they no longer wish to receive messages.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Mike Patraw using the communication channel through which you were invited or originally contacted.
